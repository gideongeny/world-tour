Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a trip?

Browse our destinations, select your preferred dates and number of guests, then complete the booking process. You'll need to create an account first.

What's included in my booking?

All bookings include accommodation, transportation, guided tours, and 24/7 support. Some packages may include meals and activities.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking up to 24 hours before departure for a full refund. Some restrictions may apply.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we recommend purchasing travel insurance for all international trips. You can add it during the booking process.
